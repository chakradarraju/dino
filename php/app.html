Tricks:
- If you don't want to post the same comment write different comments in different lines, and one of it will be posted in random to all selected posts.

- User <firstname>, <lastname>, <name> variables to leave a comment with your friend's name.
Eg: "Thanks <firstname>", will leave a comment "Thanks Chakradar"

- Use Click then Shift+Click to select a range of posts.
To load posts, choose a date and click "Fetch posts on Date"
